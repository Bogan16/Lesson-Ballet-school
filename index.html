About us

Ballet is the art of charm and sophistication.
Our ballet school provides an opportunity to learn professional ballet for both younger students and adults. Our trainers have a high level of skills and professionalism in their field. The ballet school “Darly” is equipped with a professional ballet stage covering, modern sound and lighting equipment
Our students take first place in tournaments and performances. They have the opportunity to travel around the world and play in performances on the big stage.
